The Legend of Zelda (1986) With Custom Mechanic and Level

For this project, I worked mostly on the enemies, the room/camera mechanics, and player mechanics. I also did a little bit of work on the weapons, UI, collectables, and level design. My partner did more work on the custom mechanic while I worked more on the custom level design. We both contributed in part to the custom level and the custom mechanic.

This project was made using Unity and C#.
